# AI Customer Support Auto-Responder

## Problem  
Companies struggle with slow customer support response times.

## Solution  
An AI workflow that reads incoming emails, summarizes them, and sends a professional AI-generated response.

## Workflow Logic  
- Gmail Trigger captures new emails  
- OpenAI summarizes the message  
- OpenAI generates a polite reply  
- Gmail sends it automatically  

## Benefits  
- Reduces reply time by 90%  
- Works 24/7  
- Handles repetitive support questions
